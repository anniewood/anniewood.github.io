About Me
Annie Wood

To me, post-production is the most important part of the filmmaking process. It can bring life to a potentially uninteresting piece of footage and the viewer can become instantly immersed by a good edit, without even realising. Being able to manipulate the way a story is perceived by the viewer by altering the footage in post-production is almost like having complete control over an audience√≠s emotions, this concept has fascinated me since my interest in media began.
